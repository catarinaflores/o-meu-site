FAQ

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and meme coin donations (just kidding—unless?).

How often is the archive updated?

We update our collection weekly to include the freshest and most cursed memes out there.

Can I submit my own memes?

Yes, but only if they pass the sacred vibe check. We accept submissions via ✨ carrier pigeon ✨ or a humble Google Form (link on the homepage). No stolen memes from 2014 unless they’re *ironically* stolen.

Is there a rating system for memes?

Kind of. If a meme makes us laugh, cry, question existence, or ascend briefly—into the meme plane—it stays. If not, it goes to the shadow realm (aka the rejects folder).

Are these memes safe for work?

Mostly. But “safe” is a *construct*, and your boss might not get your nuanced sense of humor. We try to tag the spicy ones, but always browse responsibly.

What era of memes does this archive cover?

All of them. From prehistoric rage comics to TikTok-core absurdity. We even have an Ancient Meme History section, where Doge is still Shibe and Bad Luck Brian still can't catch a break.

Do you fact-check the memes?

Only emotionally. If the meme *feels* right, it's in. If you're looking for peer-reviewed academic content... we suggest not being here.
